{% include ship.html name="Centaur" %}'s crest pays homage to two real life carriers, {% include hms.html name="Centaur" registry="R06" %} of the Royal Navy and {% include ship.html prefix="HMCS" name="Bonaventure" registry="CVL 22" %} of the Royal Canadian Navy. From the orginal {% include ship.html name="Centaur" %}'s crest we take the colours green and white and the bow, along with the motto Quickly Fierce. From Bonaventure's crest we take the gold horseshoe.
