{% include ship.html name="Centaur" %} is part of Task Group 94.1, a detechment of Tenth Fleet assigned to patrol Victoria Station. Victoria Station represents Western and Northern Canada (British Columbia, Alberta, Saskatchewan, Yukon, The Northwest Territories, and Nunavut) and Alaska.

Along with {% include ship.html name="Centaur" %}, TG 94.1 consists of {% include hms.html name="Callisto" %} and {% include ship.html prefix="GNS" name="Jason Mueller" %}. The other two chapters are primarily based in the lower mainland, but all three accept members from across Victoria Station.
