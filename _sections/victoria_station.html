{% include ship.html name="Centaur" %} is the flag ship of Victoria Station, a detechment of Tenth Fleet. The station represents Western and Northern Canada (British Columbia, Alberta, Saskatchewan, Yukon, The Northwest Territories, and Nunavut) and Alaska.

The other ship assigned to Victoria Station is {% include hms.html name="Callisto" %} which is based primarily in the lower mainland although both ships accept members from across the station.
